torchlib.Heap

torchlib.Heap:maxHeapify(i, effectiveSize)

Recursively swaps down the node at i until the max heap condition is restored at a[i]. Note: this function assumes that the binary trees rooted at left and right are max heaps but a[i] may violate the max-heap condition
